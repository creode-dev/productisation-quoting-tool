# Creode Team Portal User Guide

Welcome to the Creode Team Portal! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features available to team members.

## Overview

The Creode Team Portal is your central hub for managing your employee profile, documents, holiday requests, and viewing team calendars. It provides a streamlined way to handle HR-related tasks and stay connected with your team.

## Accessing the Portal

1. Log in to the application using your `@creode.co.uk` email address
2. Click "Team Portal" in the sidebar
3. You'll be taken to your portal dashboard

## Portal Sections

The portal is organized into four main sections accessible via tabs at the top:

### 1. Profile

Your employee profile contains your personal information and work details.

#### Viewing Your Profile

- Your name, email, and role are displayed
- You can see which team you're assigned to
- Your employee ID and other relevant information is shown

#### Updating Your Profile

If you need to update your profile information, contact your administrator or HR team, as profile updates may require administrative access.

### 2. Documents

The Documents section allows you to upload and manage your employee documents.

#### Uploading Documents

1. Click on the "Documents" tab
2. Click "Upload Document" or drag and drop files into the upload area
3. Select the file you want to upload (PDF, Word, Excel, or image files)
4. Enter a document name/description
5. Click "Upload"

**Note:** File size limit is currently 10MB per file.

#### Viewing Documents

- All your uploaded documents are listed in the Documents section
- Click on a document name to download it
- Documents are stored securely in Google Drive

#### Document Management

- Documents are organized by upload date
- You can see when each document was uploaded
- Contact your administrator if you need to delete a document

### 3. Holidays

The Holidays section is where you request time off and view your holiday requests.

#### Holiday Entitlement

At the top of the portal, you'll see your holiday entitlement summary:
- **Remaining Days** - Days you have left to take
- **Pending Days** - Days in requests awaiting approval
- **Annual Entitlement** - Your total annual holiday allowance
- **Pro-rata Entitlement** - Your adjusted entitlement if you started mid-year
- **Used Days** - Days you've already taken

#### Requesting Holiday

1. Click on the "Holidays" tab
2. Fill out the holiday request form:
   - **Start Date** - Select when your holiday begins
   - **End Date** - Select when your holiday ends
   - **Notes** (optional) - Add any additional information
3. Click "Submit Request"

The system will:
- Check for conflicts with existing holidays
- Verify you have enough remaining days
- Send the request to your approver
- Create a calendar event in your Google Calendar (if configured)

#### Viewing Your Holiday Requests

Below the request form, you'll see a list of all your holiday requests with:
- **Status** - Pending, Approved, or Rejected
- **Dates** - Start and end dates
- **Days** - Number of days requested
- **Submitted** - When you submitted the request

#### Holiday Request Statuses

- **Pending** - Awaiting approval from your manager
- **Approved** - Your request has been approved
- **Rejected** - Your request has been rejected (check notes for reason)

### 4. Calendar

The Calendar section shows a visual calendar of holidays for your team.

#### Viewing the Team Calendar

1. Click on the "Calendar" tab
2. You'll see a monthly calendar view
3. Holidays are displayed as colored blocks:
   - Your holidays appear in one color
   - Team members' holidays appear in another color
   - Hover over a holiday to see details

#### Calendar Features

- Navigate between months using the arrow buttons
- Click on a date to see all holidays on that day
- View which team members are on holiday
- See overlapping holidays

## Admin Features

If you have administrative access, you'll also see additional options:

### Team Management

Access via `/portal/teams` to:
- Create and manage teams
- Assign team members
- Set team approvers for holiday requests

### Employee Management

Access via `/portal/employees` to:
- View all employees
- Create new employee profiles
- Assign employees to teams
- Update employee information

### Holiday Approval Queue

Access via `/portal/approvals` to:
- View all pending holiday requests
- Approve or reject holiday requests
- See holiday request details and conflicts

## Best Practices

### Holiday Requests

1. **Plan Ahead** - Submit holiday requests as early as possible, especially for popular dates
2. **Check Availability** - Review the team calendar before requesting to avoid conflicts
3. **Provide Context** - Use the notes field to explain special circumstances if needed
4. **Track Your Balance** - Regularly check your remaining holiday days

### Document Management

1. **Use Descriptive Names** - Name your documents clearly so they're easy to find later
2. **Keep Documents Updated** - Upload new versions when documents change
3. **Organize by Type** - Consider including document type in the name (e.g., "Contract_2024.pdf")

### Calendar Usage

1. **Check Before Requesting** - Always check the calendar before submitting holiday requests
2. **Coordinate with Team** - Be aware of when team members are on holiday
3. **Plan Coverage** - Consider team coverage when requesting holidays during busy periods

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Upload Documents

- Check that your file is under 10MB
- Ensure you're using a supported file format (PDF, Word, Excel, images)
- Verify you have an active internet connection
- Contact your administrator if issues persist

### Holiday Request Not Submitting

- Verify all required fields are filled in
- Check that your end date is after your start date
- Ensure you have enough remaining holiday days
- Check for any validation error messages

### Calendar Not Loading

- Refresh the page
- Check that you're assigned to a team
- Verify your internet connection
- Contact your administrator if the issue persists

### Can't See Holiday Entitlement

- Make sure your employee profile is set up correctly
- Verify you're logged in with the correct account
- Contact your administrator to check your profile configuration
